About the Artist

Creating Visual Narratives

I'm Suryansh Mishra, a contemporary visual artist passionate about capturing the essence of human experience through realistic portraits, digital illustrations, and concept art. My work explores the delicate balance between timeless moments and emotional depth.

With over eight years of experience, I've had the privilege of working with diverse clients—from individual portrait commissions to concept art for independent projects. Each piece I create is an opportunity to tell a story, evoke emotion, and connect with viewers on a profound level.

Artistic Journey

My artistic journey began in traditional media, studying classical techniques before transitioning into hyper-realism. This foundation allows me to bring a unique perspective to contemporary illustration, combining time-honored principles with visual representation of emotions.

Recognition & Exhibitions

Secured first position in a National level competition (2023), Exhibited in Singapore for Environmental Abstract Art Showcase (2019)

My Creative Process

Every project follows a carefully considered process to ensure the final artwork exceeds expectations.

Initial Consultation

We'll discuss your vision, preferences, and requirements to ensure I fully understand your expectations for the project.

Concept Development

I'll create preliminary sketches and concepts for your review, allowing for feedback and adjustments early in the process.

Refinement

Based on your feedback, I'll refine the chosen concept, focusing on composition, details, and overall aesthetic.

Final Execution

The approved concept is brought to life with meticulous attention to detail, using the highest quality materials and techniques.
